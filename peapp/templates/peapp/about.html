    FAQ

    What can I learn on this website?

    You will be able to learn the basics of programming on GPGPU (General Purpose Graphics Processing Units) using the CUDA toolkit.

    Do I need any knowledge prerequisites?

    It is required to have basic knowledge of computer architecture and a good understanding of C-like programming languages.

    What will I get by creating an account?

    You will be able to keep track of which topics you have read, favourited and tested yourself on.

    How can I favourite a topic?

    You can favourite a topic by clicking the heart icon on the bottom right corner of the topic page. You can unfavourite a topic by clicking on the same heart icon. If the icon is gray, that means you have not favourited the topic. If the icon is blue, that means you have favourited the topic.

    Where can I see the topics I have favourited?

    Your favourite topics are visible in the Favourites tab at the top of the screen.

    How do I take an exam on a topic?

    You can take an exam by clicking on the light purple button on the bottom left corner of the screen.

    Where can I see what results I have achieved on an exam for a certain topic?

    Your exam results are visible in the My results tab at the top of the screen.
